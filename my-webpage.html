Historical places in my city

These are some of the historical places in my city:

Castle of good hope

  • The first stone was laid on 2 January 1666
  • In 1936 the Castle of Good Hope was declared a national monument
  • The Castle was never attacked.

City hall

  • City Hall is the first place from where Nelson Mandela spoke after his release.
  • The Hall itself is made up of components from all over the world.
  • The faces of the clock on the city hall are made from four skeleton iron.
